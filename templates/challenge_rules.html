Challenge Rules & Guidelines

Please read and understand these rules before creating or accepting challenges

1. General Challenge Rules

All challenges on ChallengeHub must follow these fundamental rules:

  • Challenges must be sports-related and conducted in a fair, competitive manner
  • All participants must be registered users of ChallengeHub
  • Challenges must be created with clear, specific terms and conditions
  • Participants must agree to the challenge terms before accepting
  • All matches must be conducted at approved sports grounds

2. Challenge Creation Guidelines

When creating a challenge, you must:

  • Provide clear description of the challenge format and rules
  • Specify the date, time, and location of the challenge
  • Set appropriate skill level requirements
  • Define scoring system and winning conditions
  • Include any special equipment or requirements
  • Set reasonable time limits for challenge completion

3. Fair Play & Sportsmanship

All participants must maintain the highest standards of sportsmanship:

  • Respect all opponents and officials
  • Follow the rules of the specific sport
  • Accept decisions made by officials or moderators
  • Report any violations or disputes immediately
  • Maintain a positive attitude throughout the challenge
  • Help create a welcoming environment for all participants

4. Prohibited Activities

The following activities are strictly prohibited:

  • Cheating, match-fixing, or manipulating results
  • Using performance-enhancing substances
  • Harassment, intimidation, or unsportsmanlike conduct
  • Creating fake accounts or impersonating others
  • Sharing account credentials with other users
  • Exploiting technical glitches or system vulnerabilities
  • Spamming or sending unsolicited messages

5. Challenge Acceptance & Participation

When accepting a challenge, you agree to:

  • Participate in good faith and follow all stated rules
  • Arrive on time and be prepared for the challenge
  • Bring any required equipment or materials
  • Respect the challenge format and scoring system
  • Accept the final results as determined by officials
  • Report any rule violations or disputes promptly

6. Dispute Resolution

In case of disputes or rule violations:

  • Report issues immediately to ChallengeHub moderators
  • Provide evidence and documentation when possible
  • Cooperate with any investigation process
  • Accept the final decision of ChallengeHub administrators
  • Appeal decisions through proper channels if necessary

7. Safety & Health Guidelines

All participants must prioritize safety:

  • Ensure you are physically fit to participate
  • Use appropriate safety equipment as required
  • Follow all safety protocols at sports grounds
  • Report any injuries or safety concerns immediately
  • Respect weather conditions and ground conditions
  • Follow COVID-19 and health guidelines as applicable

8. Scoring & Results

Challenge results and scoring:

  • All scores must be verified by officials or witnesses
  • Results are final once officially recorded
  • Disputes must be raised within 24 hours of completion
  • ChallengeHub reserves the right to review and adjust results
  • Performance statistics will be updated based on verified results

9. Penalties & Consequences

Violations of these rules may result in:

  • Warning or temporary suspension from challenges
  • Loss of points or ranking adjustments
  • Temporary or permanent account suspension
  • Ban from specific sports or challenge types
  • Legal action for serious violations

10. Updates to Rules

ChallengeHub reserves the right to update these rules at any time. Users will be notified of significant changes, and continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of updated rules.

Important Notice

By creating or accepting any challenge on ChallengeHub, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to abide by these rules. Failure to comply may result in penalties or account suspension.
