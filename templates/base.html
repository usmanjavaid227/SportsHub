Challenge Rules & Guidelines
1. General Challenge Rules
  • Challenges must be sports-related and conducted in a fair, competitive manner
  • All participants must be registered users of SportsHub
  • Challenges must be created with clear, specific terms and conditions
  • Participants must agree to the challenge terms before accepting
  • All matches must be conducted at approved sports grounds
2. Challenge Creation Guidelines
  • Provide clear description of the challenge format and rules
  • Specify the date, time, and location of the challenge
  • Set appropriate skill level requirements
  • Define scoring system and winning conditions
  • Include any special equipment or requirements
  • Set reasonable time limits for challenge completion
3. Fair Play & Sportsmanship
  • Respect all opponents and officials
  • Follow the rules of the specific sport
  • Accept decisions made by officials or moderators
  • Report any violations or disputes immediately
  • Maintain a positive attitude throughout the challenge
  • Help create a welcoming environment for all participants
4. Prohibited Activities
  • Cheating, match-fixing, or manipulating results
  • Using performance-enhancing substances
  • Harassment, intimidation, or unsportsmanlike conduct
  • Creating fake accounts or impersonating others
  • Sharing account credentials with other users
  • Exploiting technical glitches or system vulnerabilities
5. Challenge Types
  • Single Wicket: Team-based challenges with specific participants
  • Batting Challenge: Individual batting performance challenges
  • Bowling Challenge: Individual bowling performance challenges
6. Dispute Resolution

All disputes should be reported through the platform's dispute resolution system. Our moderators will review and make final decisions on all disputes.
