Challenge Rules & Guidelines
1. Challenge Creation Rules
  • You can only have one active challenge at a time
  • Complete or cancel your current challenge before creating a new one
  • All challenge details must be accurate and complete
  • Challenge dates must be scheduled at least 24 hours in advance
2. Challenge Types
  • Single Wicket: Team-based challenges with specific participants
  • Batting Challenge: Individual batting performance challenges
  • Bowling Challenge: Individual bowling performance challenges
3. Participant Requirements
  • All participants must have complete profiles
  • Participants must be registered users of SportsHub
  • Single wicket challenges require exactly 4 participants (2 teams of 2)
  • Regular challenges can be open to any registered user
4. Challenge Metrics
  • Metrics must be measurable and fair
  • Target values should be realistic and achievable
  • Overs count must be appropriate for the challenge type
  • All metrics are optional for Single Wicket challenges
5. Ground and Scheduling
  • Ground selection must be accurate and available
  • Time slots are managed by administrators
  • Contact administrators if time slots are not available
  • Challenge dates cannot be changed once accepted
6. Challenge Acceptance
  • Challenges can be accepted by eligible participants
  • Once accepted, challenges cannot be cancelled without mutual agreement
  • Participants must confirm their availability before accepting
  • No-shows will result in automatic challenge completion for the present participant
7. Fair Play and Conduct
  • All participants must maintain good sportsmanship
  • Cheating or unsportsmanlike conduct will result in account suspension
  • Respect all participants regardless of skill level
  • Follow all local laws and regulations
8. Dispute Resolution
  • Disputes should be reported to administrators immediately
  • Administrators have final authority on all challenge decisions
  • Evidence should be provided when reporting disputes
  • Decisions made by administrators are final
Important: By creating a challenge, you agree to abide by these rules and guidelines. Violations may result in account restrictions or suspension.
